To be eligible for a refund, you must request it within 7 days from the date of purchase.

To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at info@autovinscan.online. Provide order details and reason for your refund request.

Once your refund request is received, our team will assess it and notify you of the approval or rejection. If approved, the refund will be processed within 5-10 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

VIN reports that have been processed and delivered are non-refundable. Requests beyond 7 days are not eligible.

If you cancel before the VIN report is processed, you may receive a full refund. After processing, it is non-refundable.
